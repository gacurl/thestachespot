About Us

Our Mission

The Stache Spot is a place where people become legendary. Although you may consider a haircut just a ‘lowering of your ears’ or ‘clean up the sides’, we believe that if you look good, we look good. And if you don’t look good, then we don’t. Our mission is to change your look and your life. Become legendary at The Stache Spot.

And we're part of making the world a better place by partnering with local charities and organizations that want to make Hampton Roads legendary.

Charities & Community Partners

Locks of Love Returning Confidence to Children

Locks of Love is devoted to helping every child suffering from medical hair loss, thus we do not discriminate as to the cause of hair loss.

Haircuts4homeless Buoying Self-Esteem in Homeless

Homeless people have many challenges to face, one of them often being a low self-esteem. A haircut can change a person's life.

St. Jude Children's Hospital Finding Cures. Saving Children.

The mission of St. Jude Children’s Research Hospital is to advance cures, and means of prevention, for pediatric catastrophic diseases through research and treatment. No child is denied treatment based on race, religion or a family's ability to pay.
